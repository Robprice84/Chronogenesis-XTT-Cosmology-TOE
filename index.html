What is Chronogenesis?

Chronogenesis is an advanced theoretical framework evolving from Expanse-Time Theory (XTT). It redefines time as a quantized scalar field, Θ(x, t), whose curvature produces gravity, particle masses, energy, quantum effects, and cosmic structure. Unlike traditional theories, Chronogenesis unifies physics through a single geometric principle.
